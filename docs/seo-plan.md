# SEO Plan for SkinChange.dk

## Overview
SkinChange.dk is a Danish dermatology app offering remote consultations via image upload and questionnaire.

## Current Status
- Domain: skinchange.dk
- Language: Danish
- App available on Google Play and App Store
- Process: Upload images → answer questions → pay → dermatologists review → diagnosis within 48 hours with second opinion

## Key Pages to Optimize
1. Homepage (https://www.skinchange.dk/da)
2. How it works page
3. FAQ page
4. Privacy policy
5. App download pages (Google Play, App Store)

## Keyword Research (Danish)
Potential keywords:
- hudlæge online
- hudkonsultation
- skinchange app
- hudlidelse diagnose
- modermærkekræft opsporing
- hudforandringer scanning
- dermatolog online
- billeddiagnostik hud

## On-Page SEO Tasks
- Meta titles and descriptions
- Heading structure (H1, H2, H3)
- Image alt texts
- Internal linking
- Content optimization for target keywords

## Technical SEO
- Site speed
- Mobile responsiveness
- Structured data (MedicalWebPage, HealthTopicContent)
- XML sitemap
- Robots.txt

## Content Ideas

- Patient stories

- How-to guides (taking good skin photos)

## Competitor Analysis
Identify competing tele-dermatology services in Denmark.

## Next Steps
1. Crawl website to assess current SEO status
